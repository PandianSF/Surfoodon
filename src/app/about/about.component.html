About us!

Our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision:to alleviate hunger and food waste simultaneously. Every year, millions of tons of perfectly edible food go to waste, while countless individuals and families struggle to find their next meal. This paradox inspired us to create a solution that not only prevents food from going to landfills but also nourishes the hungry and vulnerable in our communities.

How it works:

Our platform provides an easy and effective way for individuals, restaurants, supermarkets, and businesses to donate their surplus food. Through a user-friendly interface, donors can list available food items and specify their location and pick-up times. On the other end, registered charities, food banks, and community organizations can search for nearby food donations and schedule pick-ups to distribute among those in need.
